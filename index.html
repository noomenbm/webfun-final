Risk checks

Three red flags worth slowing down for

The calculator gives you a recommendation, but these warning signs are still worth judging with your own experience.

Low budget with high urgency

If the timeline is rushed and the client budget is already tight, the project can become stressful and underpaid very quickly.

Too many moving parts

SEO, CMS setup, accessibility, design support, and extra revision rounds can turn a small project into a bigger engagement.

Vague deliverables

If you cannot describe exactly what will be delivered, you should tighten the scope before you say yes.
